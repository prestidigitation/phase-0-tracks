Guinea Pig Facts

Guinea pigs aren't from Guinea and they aren't pigs.

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Subkingdom: Bilateria
  • Infrakingdom: Deuterostomia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Subphylum: Vertebrata
  • Infraphylum: Gnathostomata
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Subclass: Theria
  • Infraclass: Eutheria
  • Order: Rodentia
  • Suborder: Hystricomorpha
  • Infraorder: Hystricognathi
  • Family: Caviidae
  • Subfamily: Caviinae
  • Genus: Cavia
  • Species: Cavia porcellus (domesticated guinea pig)
